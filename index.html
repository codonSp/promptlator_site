FAQ

Answers in 60 seconds

Is Promptlator free?

Core features are free. Optional AI-powered enhancements use your own Gemini API key.

Does it store my prompts?

Prompts stay in your browser (chrome.storage.local). Nothing is sent to our servers.

Which sites are supported?

ChatGPT (full), Claude (stub), Gemini (stub). Add-ons are adapter-based so new sites are easy.

Will it slow down my browser?

No. It’s lightweight vanilla JS with programmatic injection and Shadow DOM isolation.
